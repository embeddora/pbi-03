\subsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$class T$>$class Singleton$<$ T $>$}

Any class that inherits from \hyperlink{classSingleton}{Singleton} become itself a singleton.

\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
\hyperlink{classSingleton}{Singleton} can't be copied and can't be instanciated (new).
\end{DoxyRemark}
